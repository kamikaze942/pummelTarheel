The following program is a game dedicated to the rivalry between UNC AND DUKE

Concept was originally taken from Pummel the Chimp.  

Changes that have been made are

1.  Changed background to 640x480.
2.  Added cameron indoor stadium as background image.
3.  Replaced fist from 'pummel the chimp' to a black fist, representing Gerald Henderson. 
4.  Replaced Chimp graphic to Tyler Hansbrough, one of the most 'loved'  UNC players.
5.  Replaced text with how many tiemes player has hit tyler hansbrough. 
6.  Made Tyler Hansbrough's face shrink with every sucessive hit (1-5).
7.  Changed Sound effects to make Tyler Hansbrough cry like a girl everytime he was hit, and the 'miss' sound is homer simpson's 'doh!'
8.  Put timer in bottom right, and stop at 3 minutes.
9.  Window title has changed to 'pummel the tarheel'
10.  Allow Quit(q), Replay(r), and Restart(e).
